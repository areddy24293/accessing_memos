We are conveniently located in the heart of Cityville, making our campus easily accessible to students from both within the city and surrounding areas. Situated amidst lush greenery and providing a serene environment for learning, The Montessorie College offers a picturesque and inspiring setting for students to thrive. Our campus is equipped with modern facilities, ensuring a comfortable and conducive learning atmosphere. Feel free to reach out to us or visit our website for more information and to schedule a campus tour. We look forward to welcoming you to The Montessorie College!
